World Cup Breakout
Mbappe was selected to the star studded French World Cup lineup. Mbappe was able to prove his worth to start in the first knockout game, the quarter finals. France was up against Argentina, a tough opponent who made it to the previous World Cup Finals. Argentina also had Messi, arguably the best player in the world. At the start of the game Mbappe received the ball at his feet, and made an incredible sprint to the penalty box forcing the Argentine defender to foul him. This foul was a penalty which France scored. That run helped the world realize how great of a footballer Mbappe is. Mbappe went on to score the game sealing goal against Argentina to advance to the next round.
World Champions
Mbappe ended up helping France beat Uruguay and Belgium in the next two rounds. France ended up advancing to the World Cup finals facing Luka Modric. Luka Modric was regarded as the best player of the tournament, in his success bringing the Croatians, an underdog team, to the finals. Mbappe helped score the winning goal!
